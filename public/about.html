About Me

Hello! I'm Chanté Miles, a 21-year-old creative enthusiast with a passion for all things artistic. My journey began with a formal education in film and television at Open Window, where I honed my skills in visual storytelling. After that, I embarked on a year-long adventure into the world of tattoo artistry, adding another layer to my creative repertoire.

Creativity flows through every aspect of my life. Whether it's crafting compelling narratives, designing intricate tattoos, or capturing moments through photography, I thrive on bringing ideas to life. I’ve had the privilege of working on numerous marketing and social media projects, which have equipped me with a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of digital engagement.

In collaboration with my father, I run a small photography business. This venture has not only strengthened our bond but also allowed me to explore the beauty of capturing moments that matter.

Recently, I decided to take on a new challenge: web development. Frustrated with the difficulty of conveying my vision to web developers, I enrolled in a course to gain the skills needed to build my own websites. Now, I'm excited to combine my creative talents with technical expertise to create unique and personal online experiences.

Thank you for visiting my website. I look forward to connecting with you and sharing more of my creative journey.
